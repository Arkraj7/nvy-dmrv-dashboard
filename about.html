Vision for a Viksit Bharat & Sustainability

India’s commitment to its Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs) requires decentralized, data-driven action. Urban forestry via Nagar Vans and Nagar Vatikas represents a crucial strategy to create carbon sinks, combat the Urban Heat Island effect, and foster community resilience.

This platform empowers citizens, local authorities, and policymakers to monitor real-time metrics, effectively driving us collectively towards a truly sustainable, developed nation—a Viksit Bharat.
