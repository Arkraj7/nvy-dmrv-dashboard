Cookie Policy

Nagar Van Digital Monitoring, Reporting & Verification

Last updated: May 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies and local storage are small data files stored on your browser that help websites function efficiently and remember user preferences.

2. How We Use Them

The Nagar Van DMRV Dashboard uses strictly necessary local storage to enhance your user experience. For example:

Session Management

We use local session storage to remember if you have already seen and closed the "Acknowledgment & Data Disclaimer" popup, ensuring you are not repeatedly interrupted during a single browsing session.

Offline Functionality

We utilize service workers and caching to allow the dashboard and forms to load efficiently, even in areas with poor internet connectivity (like inside dense urban forests).

3. Third-Party Tracking

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking scripts. Your activity on this dashboard is not monetized or tracked for marketing purposes.
