Accessibility Statement

Nagar Van Digital Monitoring, Reporting & Verification

Our Commitment

The Nagar Van DMRV initiative is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all citizens, including individuals with disabilities. We believe that civic participation in climate action should be inclusive and barrier-free.

Steps We Have Taken

We are actively working to align this dashboard with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1. Current features include:

Bilingual Support

Core public interfaces, such as the public survey, are provided in both English and Hindi.

Color Contrast

The dashboard utilizes high-contrast green and gray palettes to ensure text is readable against backgrounds.

Mobile Responsiveness

The interface is designed to be fully navigable and readable on all smartphone devices, recognizing that most field data and citizen engagement occurs via mobile.

Feedback

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. If you encounter any barriers while using this dashboard or have suggestions for improvement, please reach out to us. Your feedback is vital to building a more inclusive platform.
