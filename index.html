About This Study

This study is comprehensive to understand the essence of Nagar Vans and Nagar Vatikas. It aims to evaluate their true impact on urban ecology, biodiversity, and community well-being, driving grassroots policy change and promoting sustainable urban environments.

Metrics Importance & Formulas

Live Survey Samples:
Real-time count of inputs reflecting active community and field-level engagement.
Air Quality (PM2.5):
Measures fine particulate matter mitigation. Vital for assessing public health benefits of urban forests.
Carbon Sequestration:
Formula: Biomass × 0.47. Indicates the total CO₂ equivalent absorbed, crucial for climate action goals.
Urban Heat Reduction:
Formula: ΔT (°C) Local vs Surrounding. Highlights micro-climate cooling to combat Urban Heat Islands.
Water Recharged:
Formula: Catchment Area × Rainfall × Runoff Coefficient. Shows contribution to local water tables.
Ecological Index:
Composite scale (0-10) assessing native tree density, species richness, and habitat quality.
Socio-Economic:
Evaluates non-timber forest product utility, eco-tourism potential, and vendor livelihoods generated.
Avg. WTP (CVM):
Contingent Valuation Method. The average monetary value citizens are willing to pay for maintenance.

Arkraj Biswas

Research Associate & Environmental Policy Advocate

Welcome! This initiative is a stepping stone towards actionable grassroots implementation and driving localized policy change. My core motivation is bridging the gap between national environmental goals and on-ground ecological realities.

Academic & Professional Background

I hold a Master of Science in Environmental and Sustainable Development (Earth and Atmospheric Sciences) from Banaras Hindu University, and a Bachelor of Science in Agricultural Sciences. As the founder of the Ecoserve Development Foundation and through my ongoing work with the Embark India Development Fellowship placed at National CAMPA, my focus revolves around the Nagar Van Yojana, ecosystem restoration, and building robust Digital Monitoring, Reporting, and Verification (DMRV) tools.

Vision for a Viksit Bharat & Sustainability

India’s commitment to its Nationally Determined Contributions (NDCs) requires decentralized, data-driven action. Urban forestry via Nagar Vans and Nagar Vatikas represents a crucial strategy to create carbon sinks, combat the Urban Heat Island effect, and foster community resilience. This platform empowers citizens, local authorities, and policymakers to monitor real-time metrics, driving us collectively towards a truly sustainable and developed nation.
